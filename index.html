📋 Step-by-Step Umrah

Follow each step in order for a complete, valid Umrah

Your Progress

0 of 7 steps0%
1

Entering the State of Iḥrām

At the Miqat boundary
›

Before crossing the Miqat, purify yourself, wear the Ihram garments, and make the intention (Niyyah) for Umrah. Men wear two unstitched white cloths; women wear regular modest clothing covering all except face and hands.

لَبَّيْكَ اللَّهُمَّ عُمْرَةً
Labbayka Allahumma 'umratan
"Here I am, O Allah, for Umrah." — The Niyyah
لَبَّيْكَ اللَّهُمَّ لَبَّيْكَ، لَبَّيْكَ لَا شَرِيكَ لَكَ لَبَّيْكَ
Labbayka Allahumma labbayk, labbayka la shareeka laka labbayk
The Talbiyah — recite continuously until Tawaf begins
💡 Perform Ghusl, trim nails, and remove unwanted hair before entering Ihram.
2

Entering Masjid al-Haram

The Grand Mosque
›

Enter with your right foot first. When you first see the Ka'bah, stop and make sincere dua — this is a moment of acceptance.

اللَّهُمَّ افْتَحْ لِي أَبْوَابَ رَحْمَتِكَ
Allahumma iftah li abwaba rahmatik
"O Allah, open for me the doors of Your mercy."
🕌 First sight of the Ka'bah is a moment of accepted dua — make your most sincere request.
3

Tawaf — 7 Circuits Around the Ka'bah

Counter-clockwise from the Black Stone
›

Begin at the Black Stone. Complete 7 counter-clockwise circuits. Men: expose right shoulder (Idtibaa), walk briskly in first 3 rounds (Raml). Kiss or point to the Black Stone saying "Allahu Akbar" each pass.

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ اللَّهُ أَكْبَرُ
Bismillah, Allahu Akbar
Said at the Black Stone each circuit
رَبَّنَا آتِنَا فِي الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةً وَفِي الْآخِرَةِ حَسَنَةً وَقِنَا عَذَابَ النَّارِ
Rabbana atina fid-dunya hasanatan wa fil-akhirati hasanatan wa qina adhab an-nar
Between Yemeni Corner and Black Stone
⚡ Use our Tawaf Mode in the map section to follow each circuit with specific duas.
4

Prayer at Maqam Ibrahim

2 Rakat behind the Station of Ibrahim
›

Pray 2 Rakat behind Maqam Ibrahim — recite Surah Al-Kafirun in the first and Al-Ikhlas in the second. Then drink Zamzam water.

وَاتَّخِذُوا مِن مَّقَامِ إِبْرَاهِيمَ مُصَلًّى
Wattakhidhu min maqami Ibrahima musalla
"And take the station of Ibrahim as a place of prayer." (2:125)
💧 After the 2 Rakat, go to Zamzam well. Drink facing the Ka'bah making dua.
5

Sa'i — Between Safa & Marwa

7 walks, starting at Safa
›

Begin at Safa, walk to Marwa — one lap. Complete 7 laps, ending at Marwa. Men jog between the green lights. Commemorates Hajar's search for water.

إِنَّ الصَّفَا وَالْمَرْوَةَ مِن شَعَائِرِ اللَّهِ
Innas-Safa wal-Marwata min sha'a'irillah
Recited ascending Safa (Al-Baqarah 2:158)
🏃 The Sa'i corridor is air-conditioned and wheelchair accessible on the ground floor.
6

Halq or Taqsir — Cutting Hair

Men shave/shorten; women trim slightly
›

Men shave the head (Halq — more virtuous) or shorten all around (Taqsir). Women cut a fingertip length. This releases you from Ihram.

✂️ After this step all Ihram restrictions are lifted. You may wear regular clothes and use perfume.
7

🎉 Umrah Complete!

Make dua and give thanks
›

Alhamdulillah — your Umrah is complete! Make sincere dua for yourself, family, and the Ummah. Perform optional Tawaf al-Wida before leaving Makkah.

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي بِنِعْمَتِهِ تَتِمُّ الصَّالِحَاتُ
Alhamdulillahi alladhi bi ni'matihi tatimmus salihaat
"All praise is for Allah, by Whose blessings good deeds are completed."
تَقَبَّلَ اللَّهُ مِنَّا وَمِنكُم
May Allah accept from us and from you.

📖 History & Significance

The sacred stories behind the holy sites

🕋
~2000 BCE · Foundation

The Ka'bah — House of Allah

Built by Prophet Ibrahim ﷺ and his son Ismail ﷺ as the first house of worship. As they laid the stones, they prayed: "Our Lord, accept this from us. You are the All-Hearing, the All-Knowing." (2:127). The Ka'bah is the qiblah — the direction Muslims face in all prayers worldwide.

⬛
Pre-Islamic · Sacred Stone

Hajar al-Aswad — The Black Stone

The Black Stone descended from Paradise white as milk but turned black from the sins of mankind. The Prophet ﷺ said: "The Black Stone descended from Paradise." It marks the start and end of each Tawaf circuit and is kissed or pointed to as an act of reverence.

👣
~2000 BCE · Miracle

Maqam Ibrahim — Station of Ibrahim

When Ibrahim ﷺ stood on a stone to build the Ka'bah, Allah softened it — leaving his footprints preserved within. The Quran commands: "Take the station of Ibrahim as a place of prayer." (2:125).

💧
~2000 BCE · Water of Life

Zamzam Well

When Ibrahim ﷺ left Hajar and infant Ismail in the barren valley, Hajar ran desperately between Safa and Marwa searching for water. Allah answered miraculously — the Zamzam spring gushed forth and has never dried in over 4,000 years.

🏃
~2000 BCE · Act of Faith

Safa & Marwa — The Sa'i Walk

Commemorates Hajar's seven runs between the two hills in search of water for infant Ismail. Her unshakeable trust in Allah is honored by every pilgrim who walks this path today.

🕌
630 CE — Present

Masjid al-Haram — The Grand Mosque

The Prophet ﷺ entered Makkah and purified the Ka'bah of idols in 630 CE. Today the mosque covers over 356,800 m² accommodating millions during Hajj. One prayer here equals 100,000 prayers elsewhere.

🌙
610 CE · First Revelation

Cave of Hira

On Jabal al-Nour, Angel Jibreel descended with the first revelation: "Read in the name of your Lord who created." (96:1). This moment marked the beginning of the Quran and changed the world forever.

رَبَّنَا تَقَبَّلْ مِنَّا إِنَّكَ أَنتَ السَّمِيعُ الْعَلِيمُ
Our Lord, accept from us. Indeed, You are the Hearing, the Knowing.

👘 Ihram Guide

Everything you need to know before and during Ihram

وَأَتِمُّوا الْحَجَّ وَالْعُمْرَةَ لِلَّهِ
"Complete the Hajj and Umrah for the sake of Allah."
— Al-Baqarah 2:196
🛁

Before Entering Ihram

  • Perform Ghusl (full body wash) with the intention of Ihram
  • Clip nails and trim/remove unwanted hair
  • Apply perfume to your body only (not the cloth) before wearing Ihram
  • Men: wear two unstitched white cloths (Izar + Rida)
  • Women: regular modest clothing — face and hands uncovered
  • Pray 2 Rakat (if not a disliked time)
  • Face Qiblah and make the Niyyah at the Miqat
🗺️

Miqat Boundaries

  • Dhul Hulayfah — From Madinah (~9 km from Madinah)
  • Al-Juhfah / Rabigh — From Syria, Morocco, West
  • Qarn al-Manazil — From Najd, Arabian Peninsula
  • Yalamlam — From Yemen and South
  • Dhat Irq — From Iraq and East
  • By air: wear Ihram before boarding or at the Miqat announcement
🚫

Forbidden While in Ihram

  • Cutting hair or nails
  • Applying perfume or scented products
  • Sexual intercourse or intimate relations
  • Men wearing stitched clothing or shoes covering ankles
  • Men covering their head
  • Hunting or killing animals unnecessarily
  • Proposing marriage or conducting wedding contracts
  • Arguing, fighting, or using foul language
✅

Permitted While in Ihram

  • Showering with unscented soap
  • Wearing a watch, glasses, hearing aids
  • Using an umbrella for shade
  • Eating, drinking, and sleeping normally
  • Killing harmful animals in self-defense
