📋 Step-by-Step Umrah

Follow each step in order for a complete, valid Umrah

Your Progress

1

Entering the State of Iḥrām

At the Miqat boundary
›

Before crossing the Miqat, purify yourself, wear the Ihram garments, and make the intention (Niyyah) for Umrah. Men wear two unstitched white cloths; women wear regular modest clothing.

لَبَّيْكَ اللَّهُمَّ عُمْرَةً
Labbayka Allahumma 'umratan
"Here I am, O Allah, for Umrah."
لَبَّيْكَ اللَّهُمَّ لَبَّيْكَ، لَبَّيْكَ لَا شَرِيكَ لَكَ لَبَّيْكَ
Labbayka Allahumma labbayk, labbayka la shareeka laka labbayk
Talbiyah — recite continuously until Tawaf begins.
💡 Perform Ghusl before Ihram. Trim nails and remove unwanted hair beforehand.
2

Entering Masjid al-Haram

The Grand Mosque of Makkah
›

Enter with your right foot first. When you first see the Ka'bah, stop and make sincere dua — this moment is especially accepted.

اللَّهُمَّ افْتَحْ لِي أَبْوَابَ رَحْمَتِكَ
Allahumma iftah li abwaba rahmatik
"O Allah, open for me the doors of Your mercy."
🕌 First sight of the Ka'bah — your heartfelt dua is especially accepted at this moment.
3

Tawaf — Circling the Ka'bah

7 circuits counter-clockwise
›

Begin at the Black Stone (Hajar al-Aswad). Complete 7 counter-clockwise circuits. Kiss or point to the Black Stone saying "Allahu Akbar" each pass. Men walk briskly for the first 3 rounds (Raml).

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ اللَّهُ أَكْبَرُ
Bismillah, Allahu Akbar
Said at the Black Stone — beginning each circuit.
رَبَّنَا آتِنَا فِي الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةً وَفِي الْآخِرَةِ حَسَنَةً وَقِنَا عَذَابَ النَّارِ
Rabbana atina fid-dunya hasanatan wa fil-akhirati hasanatan wa qina 'adhab an-nar
Between Yemeni Corner and the Black Stone.
🕋 Use the Tawaf Mode in the Haram Map to follow each round with specific duas.
4

Prayer at Maqam Ibrahim

2 Rakat behind the Station of Ibrahim
›

After Tawaf, pray 2 Rakat behind Maqam Ibrahim. Recite Surah Al-Kafirun in the first rakat and Surah Al-Ikhlas in the second. Then drink Zamzam water.

وَاتَّخِذُوا مِن مَّقَامِ إِبْرَاهِيمَ مُصَلًّى
Wattakhidhu min maqami Ibrahima musalla
"And take the station of Ibrahim as a place of prayer." — Al-Baqarah 2:125
5

Sa'i — Walking Between Safa & Marwa

7 walks ending at Marwa
›

Begin at Mount Safa and walk to Mount Marwa — one lap. Complete 7 laps total, ending at Marwa. Men jog between the green lights.

إِنَّ الصَّفَا وَالْمَرْوَةَ مِن شَعَائِرِ اللَّهِ
Innas-Safa wal-Marwata min sha'a'irillah
"Indeed, Safa and Marwa are among the symbols of Allah." — Al-Baqarah 2:158
💧 Drink Zamzam water before beginning Sa'i. The walkway is air-conditioned.
6

Halq or Taqsir — Cutting the Hair

Men shave or shorten; women trim slightly
›

Men either shave the head completely (Halq — more virtuous) or shorten all around (Taqsir). Women cut a fingertip length. This releases you from Ihram.

✂️ Halq (full shave) is the Prophet's ﷺ act and is superior. Barbers are available near the Masa'.
7

Umrah Complete 🎉

Make dua and give thanks
›

Alhamdulillah — your Umrah is complete! Take this moment to make sincere dua for yourself, your family, and the Ummah.

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي بِنِعْمَتِهِ تَتِمُّ الصَّالِحَاتُ
Alhamdulillahi alladhi bi ni'matihi tatimmus salihaat
"All praise is for Allah, by Whose blessings good deeds are completed."
تَقَبَّلَ اللَّهُ مِنَّا وَمِنكُم

May Allah accept from us and from you.

📖 History & Significance

The sacred stories behind the holy sites

🕋
~2000 BCE · Foundation

The Ka'bah — House of Allah

Built by Prophet Ibrahim ﷺ and his son Ismail ﷺ as the first house of worship. As they laid the stones, they prayed: "Our Lord, accept this from us." (Al-Baqarah 2:127). It is the qiblah — the direction Muslims face in all prayers worldwide.

⬛
Pre-Islamic Era · Sacred Stone

Hajar al-Aswad — The Black Stone

Descended from Paradise white as milk, it turned black from the sins of mankind. It marks the beginning and end of each Tawaf circuit. The Prophet ﷺ said it will testify on the Day of Judgment for those who touched it with faith.

👣
~2000 BCE · Miracle

Maqam Ibrahim — Station of Ibrahim

When Ibrahim ﷺ stood on a stone to lay the higher courses of the Ka'bah, Allah caused it to soften, leaving his footprints preserved. The Quran commands: "Take the station of Ibrahim as a place of prayer." (Al-Baqarah 2:125).

💧
~2000 BCE · Miracle of Water

Zamzam Well — Water of Life

When Ibrahim left Hajar and infant Ismail in the valley, Hajar ran searching for water. Angel Jibreel struck the earth and the blessed Zamzam spring gushed forth. This well has never dried in over 4,000 years and remains remarkable in its purity.

🏃
~2000 BCE · Act of Faith

Safa & Marwa — The Sa'i Walk

The ritual of Sa'i commemorates Hajar's seven runs between Mount Safa and Mount Marwa in search of water. The Quran honors her act: "Indeed, Safa and Marwa are among the symbols of Allah." (Al-Baqarah 2:158).

🌙
610 CE · First Revelation

Cave of Hira — The First Revelation

On Jabal al-Nour (Mountain of Light), the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ received the first Quranic revelation: "Read in the name of your Lord who created." (Al-Alaq 96:1). This moment changed the world forever.

👘 Ihram Guide

Everything you need to know before and during Ihram

وَأَتِمُّوا الْحَجَّ وَالْعُمْرَةَ لِلَّهِ
"And complete the Hajj and Umrah for the sake of Allah."
— Al-Baqarah 2:196
🛁

Before Entering Ihram

  • Perform Ghusl (full body wash) with the intention of entering Ihram
  • Clip nails and trim/remove unwanted hair before entering Ihram
  • Apply perfume to your body only — before wearing the garment
  • Men: wear two pieces of unstitched white cloth (Izar + Rida)
  • Women: wear regular, loose, modest clothing covering all except face and hands
  • Pray 2 Rakat before making the intention
  • Face the Qiblah and make the intention (Niyyah) for Umrah at the Miqat
🗺️

Miqat Boundaries

  • Dhul Hulayfah — For those coming from Madinah
  • Al-Juhfah / Rabigh — For those from Syria, Morocco, and the West
  • Qarn al-Manazil — For those from Najd and the Arabian Peninsula
  • Yalamlam — For those from Yemen and the South
  • Dhat Irq — For those from Iraq and the East
  • Pilgrims arriving by air: wear Ihram before boarding or when the aircraft announces the Miqat
🚫

Forbidden While in Ihram

  • Cutting hair or nails from any part of the body
  • Applying perfume or scented products
  • Sexual intercourse or intimate relations
  • Men wearing stitched clothing or shoes covering the ankles
  • Men covering their head
  • Hunting or killing any animal unnecessarily
  • Proposing marriage or conducting wedding contracts
  • Arguing, fighting, or using foul language
✅

Permitted While in Ihram

  • Showering and washing with unscented soap
  • Wearing a watch, glasses, hearing aids, or medical devices
  • Using an umbrella for shade
  • Eating, drinking, and sleeping normally
  • Killing harmful animals in self-defense (snakes, scorpions)
🤲Remember: Ihram is not just garments — it's a state of spiritual elevation. Keep your heart pure, your tongue busy with dhikr, and your mind focused on Allah throughout your journey.
